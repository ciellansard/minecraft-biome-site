MINECRAFT

Minecraft is a 3D sandbox adventure game developed by Mojang Studios where players can interact with a fully customizable three-dimensional world made of blocks and entities.

FOREST BIOMES

Within Minecraft there are a large variety of forests throughout the world. These forests contain diverse ecosystems, and are the most common biomes in the game. Many basic necessities are found within these types of biomes needed to start off the game. Out of all biomes, these ones end to be more on the friendly side compared to other biomes, with it only becoming dangerous at night time, when it's dark out.

CAVE BIOMES

Many important resources are hidden away deep within the caves. Throughout the Overworld, caves are typically far more dangerous than the surface as there is typically no light, thus mobs are constantly present. These biomes are full of mysterious structures for the players to explore. Typically, these structures tend to be even more so dangerous than caves alone.

OCEAN BIOMES

One of the largest group of biomes within Minecraft. Titanic in size, the ocean biomes are home to unique aquatic creatures, friendly and hostile alike. Oceans can be easy means to discover long-forgotten loot hidden away in shipwrecked boats, underwater ruins, temples and even destroyed portals to the Nether. A sea of mysteries for the player to explore, the Ocean biomes are as wonderous as they are large, which is very large.

NETHER BIOMES

One of the most dangerous places to be, the Nether is a scorching hot dimension in Minecraft. With lava nearly everywhere, exploring this biome becomes incredibly risky, yet rewarding. Some of the game's best materials reside deep within the Nether's many structures. Although this biome is unbearably humid, it is still home to many diverse ecosystems full of life. Very cool.

END BIOMES

The final frontier of Minecraft. This is where one of the most powerful adversaries resides. This dimension is an archipelago of floating islands, surrounded by a bottomless void. Being the last and most dangerous dimension, it is incredibly difficult to explore. However the materials gained from doing so are incredibly valuable. Although at that late point in the game, the value of such tools is questionable, it's still impressive.
