Minecraft's caves are full of both mystery and resources. Each world has many caves, filled to the brim with many caverns and cavities chock-full of precious minerals and archaic structures for the player to discover. With the darkness of the caves, they are often home to plethoras of hostile mobs, making them a dangerous place to be.

MONSTER ROOM

Monster rooms are dangerous rooms scattered around the occasional cave. Within them is a monster spawner and loot.

MINESHAFT

These long abandoned mines are mazes of track and stone. Within the average mineshaft, there is tons of loot and ore.

AMETHYST GEODE

Very purple and very pretty. These geodes are full of amethyst minerals, kind of self-explanatory. The amethyst is very cool and purple.

Monolithic in size, ancient cities are a dangerous place to be. Yet they are full of incredibly valuable loot. These cities are inhabited by the strongest mob, wardens, making exploring dangerous.
